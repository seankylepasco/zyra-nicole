ABOUT US

The word "Balai" is derived from the Malay word “balay” which means house.
"Apong" is an Ilokano word which means grandparent, grandfather, or grandmother. "Balai Ni Apong" means "Bahay Ni Lola/Lolo". It is a retirement home designed by the C.O.R.E (Caregivers Of Retired Elderlies) to meet the evolving needs of senior citizens.

Balai ni Apong is a privately-run for-profit-organization. This type of home health agency is owned by Ms. Princess Caido. The company has 4 board members, Ms. Kimberly Grace Monasterial, Ms. Russell Neil Rosario, Charlene Ricci Manalansan, and Ms. Jhanarie Mison who are also the shareholders of the company. Balai ni Apong was founded in the year 2022 in the Philippines. It has a Multi-Disciplinary Team of 9 Caring Health Care Professionals, ready to serve and take care of our seniors that are in need.

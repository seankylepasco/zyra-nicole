Our Services


Services Description
MEDI-CARE PARA KAY LOLO AT LOLA • We offer assistance for the Residents with their medications, if needed with the 10 medication rights as guide.
• Medications should be brought to facility by the Resident or the responsible party or delivered by the pharmacy and billed directly to the Resident or responsible party.
• Residents will be reminded for their medication intake and will be monitored through their medication logbook.
Room and Board We offer a setting that feels like home for our elderly clients. There are regular rooms, VIP rooms, and Guests rooms for outpatient care. All furniture and bedding are provided. The Residents may bring their own furniture as long as it can accommodate.
Laundry Personal laundry service is provided at least every other day or as needed. Bed linen is washed weekly or as needed.
Personal Services Showers or baths will be provided. Personal grooming assistant is provided on daily basis. This includes dressing, bathing, toilet assistance, dental, hair care, and nail care. (The Resident may be referred to a local podiatrist when necessary.) Basic personal care supplies such as soap, shampoo, toothpaste, toilet paper, and tissue will be added to the bill of the residents
Meal Preparation Three nutritious meals are served each day with snacks available between meals and in the evening. Water, milk, and juice are offered throughout the day. The Provider will discuss personal preferences regarding meals with the resident and try to meet these preferences. Special diets will be accommodated, if needed
Transportation We offer transportation services that will allow us to take our residents from our retirement home to the hospital, in case of an emergency or in case thorough medical attention is needed. If a Resident requires transportation for other purposes, it will be the responsibility of the Resident’s family or representative to provide this service.
Water and Electric Facilities These necessities are included with the resident’s bill.
Medical Equipment for Rent TWe allow the residents to rent medical assistive devices like wheelchair, crutches, or canes. A typical loan period is 3 months, but they may be able to extend the term if needed.
Round-The-Clock Nursing Care and Companionship Services Our skilled nurses are available on site 24/7 providing help for the needs of our residents
Mental Health Services We offer mental health assistance for our residents. Residents are required to visit the mental health office and encouraged to open up about their frustrations and concerns regarding life. with a professional psychiatrist at least once a week.
Recreational and Spiritual Nursing Care We offer different recreational activities that are meaningful to the residents to enhance their quality of life. The home will attempt to determine what activities the Resident enjoyed throughout his/her life, and if possible, offer the Resident the opportunity to engage in them while living in Balai Ni Apong. Other various activities will also be offered to increase mental and physical stimulation.

We will provide everyday schedule of schedule of activities to be conducted either indoors in the center’s lounge or outdoors in the center’s garden. Elderlies are free to choose which activities they would like to participate in. We are always open to suggestions and encourage input.

Activities: Chess, bingo, life story exercises, book clubs, prayer circles, reminiscing, current news discussion, gardening, light exercise, and celebration of birthdays and other holidays
IINDAK MO (PHYSICAL ACTIVITIES) We facilitate senior exercise programs to promote the physical health of our elderly clients through walking clubs, gardening clubs, and exercise classes including Zumba and ROM exercises
AFFILIATED MEDICAL SERVICES Balai Ni Apong offers affiliated medical services for the residents including physical therapist, occupational therapist, geriatrician, cardiologist, neurologist, and nutritionist to cater residents with their specific needs.
